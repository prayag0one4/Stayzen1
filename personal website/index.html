About us

Greetings from the students of the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota! We, the students of the batch of 2023, are here to extend a helping hand to anyone facing challenges in hostel life.
At IIIT Kota, we understand that hostel life comes with its own set of joys and challenges. From navigating roommate dynamics to managing life in the hostel mess and finding your place in sports activities, we're committed to making your hostel experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
Our team consists of dedicated students who have firsthand experience living in the IIIT Kota hostels. We've been through it all – from late-night study sessions to impromptu sports matches and everything in between. Now, we're here to share our knowledge, offer support, and lend a listening ear to those in need.
